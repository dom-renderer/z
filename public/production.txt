. Before Morning Shift Starts (Order Sheet Upload)
Orders from shops / customers are already prepared in another software and come as a ready file (order sheet).
Office staff goes to the Order Upload Screen in our system.
Here the Date and Shift = Morning will be pre-selected by default.
Staff does not need to change date or shift.
Staff simply uploads the ready Order Sheet file.
The system automatically calculates for each product:
Total Orders = Sales Order Quantity + Indent Quantity (read directly from the sheet)
Opening Stock (Morning) = always ZERO (for the morning shift, opening stock is always 0, without exception)
So Production Required = Total Orders ‚Äì Opening Stock = Total Orders
This way, the full production plan for the morning shift is ready: how much to produce for each item.
